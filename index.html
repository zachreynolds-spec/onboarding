SoftPull Pre-Qualification Terms & Conditions

Positive Outcome: If a consumer meets your specified minimum credit criteria, MonitorBase will notify you via your configured alert system. You may then contact the consumer with a firm offer of credit as permitted under the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA), 15 U.S.C. § 1681 et seq., specifically under the prescreening provisions of § 1681b(c).

Negative Outcome: If a consumer does not meet your minimum criteria, no notification will be sent and no action will be taken. The consumer will not be contacted in connection with this inquiry.

Full Terms & Conditions: By participating in the MonitorBase SoftPull program, you (the "Subscriber") agree to the following:

1. FCRA Compliance: Subscriber agrees to use SoftPull data solely for permissible purposes under the FCRA, including but not limited to the prescreened firm offer of credit provisions. Subscriber will not use this data for any other purpose without separate written authorization.

2. Firm Offer Requirement: Any contact with a consumer identified through SoftPull must include a bona fide firm offer of credit as defined under the FCRA. Subscriber is solely responsible for ensuring all firm offers comply with applicable law.

3. Opt-Out Compliance: Subscriber must honor all consumer opt-out requests as required by 15 U.S.C. § 1681b(e) and must maintain procedures to remove opted-out consumers from contact lists.

4. Data Security: Subscriber agrees to maintain reasonable data security measures to protect consumer information obtained through SoftPull, consistent with the Safeguards Rule under the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act.

5. Consent to Contact: By signing below, Subscriber acknowledges and consents to MonitorBase accessing consumer credit file data for the purpose of matching against the specified FICO criteria, and authorizes MonitorBase to generate alerts and notifications on Subscriber's behalf.

6. Indemnification: Subscriber shall indemnify and hold harmless MonitorBase, its officers, directors, and employees from any claim, liability, loss, or expense arising from Subscriber's use of SoftPull data or violation of any applicable law.
